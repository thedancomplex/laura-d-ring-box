# Summary

Weighted Companion Cube from the Portal remixed for easy printing without supports, outer dimensions are 5x5x5 cm (~2x2x2 in).

It is hollow and can serve as a box for small things, the top lid has a press-fit. Use a small file or sandpaper to slightly flatten the sides if needed for desired fit.

There is also a stencil, that can be used for spray painting gray parts on each side.Just snap the stencil on a cube's side and paint, it will block white areas. You can print the whole stencil, or just about 1 cm in height and use some plastic (on the photo) to cover rest of the cube, you will save time & filament.
